About me

Hi, I'm Ryan!

I'm working towards becoming a web developer, starting with front-end development. Right now, I'm focused on learning HTML and CSS and once I feel more confident with them, I'll move on to JavaScript before learning about frameworks and libraries such as React.

I first got into coding while I was still working, learning HTML and CSS in my spare time. Unfortunately, due to medical reasons I had to leave my job. I ended up switching focus for a while and completed a Level 2 Business Administration qualification as well as learning programs like Microsoft Word, Excel and Outlook to try and get into office work, since most of my experience was in manual labour.

After I lost my job, I applied to hundreds of office roles and spoke to every local recruitment agencies I could. The response was always the same; not enough experience, even for jobs I was lucky enough to interview for. That's when I stepped back and thought seriously about what I really wanted. I realised that with web development, not only do I enjoy it, but it's also something I can grow in by building my own experience.

So now I'm putting my energy into learning web development properly. It's not easy, but it's something I enjoy and see a future in, and that makes it worth it.
